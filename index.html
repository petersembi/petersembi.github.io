MERRY CHRISTMAS

Suppose you were to celebrate someone today, what would you do? Buy gifts, talk about what him/her etc. I guess the first thing to come to your mind will not be buying clothes for yourself, go swimming but rather all efforts will be towards appreciating what he has done. Isn't that what we do during Christmas?

I'm not trying to imply that people should not have good time together, but in those celebrations, will you talk about Christ? Will unbelievers hear about Christ more during this season? Will your relationship with Christ grow? Food for thought!

Christmas is about celebrating Christ and what he did for us. He paid the price that we could not afford for the forgiveness of our sins.

Isaiah 9:6 "For to us a child is born, to us a son is given, and the government will be on his shoulders. And he will be called Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace."
